About Us

No Refunds Entertainment is a close company with many friendly staff. We pride ourselves in our work ethic and familial feeling between each other. Our CEO, Danny Seauber, created No Refunds Entertainment after an unfortunate incident which left him unable to persue his original dreams in this world. With No Refunds Entertainment, he has found his new calling and has been able to live life in a way that he is proud of. We hope to provide customers with experiences that can last a lifetime.

Barefoot Bay Leisureland seeks to provide fun for the whole family using safe and well-maintained equipment. We offer concessions, ziplining, and a child friendly water park for those who are not yet thrillseeking enough to brave the line above the crocodile pit.
