Rules and Guidelines

For your safety, Barefoot Bay Leisureland has some rules that customers must follow. Failure to follow these rules will result in removal from rides or the park.

Guidelines

  • Please abide by doctor recommentations on riding or participating in our attractions.
  • Participants must not be visibly intoxicated to ride the zipline.
  • Participants must not be pregnant to ride the zipline.
  • Pets may not ride the ziplines.
  • Close-toed shoes are required.

Rules and Instructions

  • Gently step off the platform to begin the ride.
  • Using the riding glove, gently apply pressure to the line to brake and slow down. You must apply the handbrake at the pole with the snake on it.
  • Our emergency brake spring system will take care of the rest of the braking for you! We can guarantee its safety.
